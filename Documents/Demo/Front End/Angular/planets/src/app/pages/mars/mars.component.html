Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second smallest planet in the Solar System, being larger than only Mercury. In English, Mars carries the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred ti as the "Red Planet". The latter refers to the effect of the iron oxide prevalent on Mar's surface, which gives it a reddish appearance distinctive among the astronomical bodies visible to the naked eye.
